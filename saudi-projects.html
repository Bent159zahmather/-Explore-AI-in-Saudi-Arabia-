Smart Hajj

AI systems are used to manage crowd flow and provide real-time guidance for pilgrims.

Read More

The Smart Hajj initiative in Saudi Arabia uses AI to monitor crowds, predict congestion, and ensure a safer and smoother pilgrimage experience. Smart robots and data systems help guide pilgrims and assist them in multiple languages.

NEOM City

A futuristic city where AI manages transportation, energy, and urban planning efficiently.

Read More

Detailed Summary: One of NEOM’s main projects is "The Line," a groundbreaking linear city that aims to eliminate cars and emissions by relying on AI-driven smart infrastructure for transportation, healthcare, and utilities. It reflects Saudi Arabia’s goal to develop sustainable, high-tech urban environments. Alongside this, the "Human" app (or similar digital platforms) supports this vision by using AI and data to provide smart services like health, social benefits, and personalized experiences, helping citizens stay connected and efficient.
